Brief History

The Dimwood Museum was founded by a group of ferociously loyal fans of Dungeons and Dragons. Their goal has been to create a space where existing fans could experience the game in a special way. It is also designed to welcome those that are new to the game. We are excited to see you!

New Exhibits!!!

In addition to its regular DnD historical exhibits, The Dimwood is currently featuring two interactive experiences. The first is a virtual reality journey into the Dwarvian era. The second is a costume/photo shop where visitors can choose to dress up in their favorite DnD attire and get their picture taken.
